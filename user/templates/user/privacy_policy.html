Privacy Policy

Effective Date:1 April 2025

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of our **Passport Automation System**. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our passport automation system, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Nationality, and Address.
  • Contact Information: Email address, Phone number.
  • Identity Verification Data: Aadhaar Number, Passport Number, Fingerprint or Facial Recognition data.
  • Uploaded Documents: Proof of identity, address, and supporting documents.
  • Payment Details: If applicable, we collect payment transaction details securely through authorized gateways.
  • System Information: IP address, browser type, and device information for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process and manage your passport application.
  • To verify your identity and documents using AI-based facial recognition, OCR, and government APIs.
  • To ensure the security and authenticity of submitted applications.
  • To facilitate online biometric verification (fingerprint or facial scan).
  • To provide real-time tracking of your application status.
  • To communicate with you regarding your application progress and status updates.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Security and Protection

We implement stringent security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Data encryption during transmission and storage.
  • Multi-layer authentication for account access.
  • Strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Regular security audits and compliance checks.

Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized personnel and government agencies involved in passport processing.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, your information may be shared with:

  • Government Authorities: Ministry of External Affairs, Passport Offices, and Police Departments for verification.
  • Law Enforcement Agencies: If required by law or for national security purposes.
  • Payment Providers: To process application fees securely.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitations on how your data is processed.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for processing your passport application. Once the application process is complete, your data is either securely archived or deleted in compliance with government regulations.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your user experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If changes are made, we will notify users through email or a notification on our website.
