Frequently Asked Privacy & Security Questions

You can reset your password by going to the Settings → Security & Privacy section and clicking on "Change Password." Alternatively, you can use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page.

To enable 2FA, go to Settings → Security & Privacy and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA). You can choose between Email, SMS, or an Authenticator App for verification.

You can delete your account by navigating to Settings → Account Management and selecting "Delete Account." Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

You can update your profile information by going to Settings → Profile. There, you can edit your name, email, phone number, and profile picture.

To customize your notifications, go to Settings → Notification Preferences. You can enable or disable different types of notifications, including email, SMS, and push notifications.

We accept a variety of payment methods, including Credit/Debit Cards, PayPal, UPI, and Net Banking. You can manage your payment methods under Settings → Payments.

If you need assistance, you can reach out to our support team via the "Contact Us (here)" page or email us at supportpassportX@gmail.com.
